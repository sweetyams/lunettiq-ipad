The hybrid rule

Structure and behaviour are iOS-native. Content and identity are Lunettiq.

Stays native: sheets with grabbers and detents, blur-material bars, SF Symbols, system springs, native switches and keyboards, system haptics.
Gets branded: all type, all colour, buttons (never tint-blue), editorial photo treatment, empty-state line art, 12 pt max radius with continuous curvature.
Nothing decorative moves. Motion communicates state or hierarchy only. Reduce Motion turns every spring into a 150 ms crossfade.
Photography wins. When a screen contains a product or fitting photo, the photo gets the area and the chrome shrinks around it.
